ABOUT ME

My name is Walter Carbajal. I am currently a senior student at California State University Los Angeles working towards a BS in computer science. I am a huge sci-fi fan I love everything about it. But the main thing that draws me to the genre is the innovative ways that technology can be used. The same sense of adventure and curiosity that drives sci-fi also drives me to work outside my comfort and knowledge set by continuing to learn new languages and development techniques.

I have worked on projects ranging from small personal projects to large scale projects for large institutions and companies. I mainly work on web development, but I recently have started working on machine learning and game development. Eventually, I would like to tie up all of my acquired skills and combine them to Virtual Reality development.
